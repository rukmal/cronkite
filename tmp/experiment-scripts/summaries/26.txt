Title: Siemens signs 3 bln eur train deal in India | Reuters
Date: Mon, 16 Jan 2023 13:47:00 GMT
URL: https://www.reuters.com/business/autos-transportation/siemens-signs-3-bln-eur-train-deal-india-2023-01-16/
Summary:
• Siemens signs 3 bln eur train deal in India, biggest locomotive deal in its history
• Deal includes delivery of 1,200 electric locomotives and servicing for 35 years
• Trains have 9,000-horsepower and a top speed of 120 km/hr
• Assembled in India over 11 years, with deliveries starting in 24 months
• Replaces 500,000 to 800,000 trucks over their lifecycle
• Siemens also looking at other train contracts in India
