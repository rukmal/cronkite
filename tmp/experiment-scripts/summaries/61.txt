Title: Why some cars from the 1990s are soaring in value | CNN Business
Date: Mon, 16 Jan 2023 18:12:00 GMT
URL: https://www.cnn.com/2023/01/16/business/1990s-cars-collectible/index.html
Summary:
• 1990s cars increasing in value due to modern comfort and safety features, raw mechanical feel
• Collectible cars rising in demand, usually sports cars or performance models, pickups and SUVs also becoming collectible
• Japanese sports cars from the 1980s and 1990s popular with collectors, values rising
• Radwood events series for 1980s and 1990s cars, owned and operated by Hagerty
• 25 year rule allowing cars that couldn't be owned in US when new to be imported and driven
• Cars becoming collectible and desirable, but still functioning as vehicles for daily use
