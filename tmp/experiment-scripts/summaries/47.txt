Title: Binance to let institutions store crypto with cold custody
Date: Mon, 16 Jan 2023 13:51:12 GMT
URL: https://cointelegraph.com/news/binance-to-let-institutions-store-crypto-with-cold-custody
Summary:
• Binance launches Mirror service for institutional investors to securely store crypto in cold custody
• Mirror service based on Binance Custody and involves mirroring cold-storage assets through 1:1 collateral held on a Binance account
• Binance Custody secured cold-wallet insurance in Lithuania to operate an institutional-grade digital asset custody solution
• Growing trend of self-custody as investors flock away from centralized exchanges
