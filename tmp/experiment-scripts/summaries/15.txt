Title: Davos: PwC survey finds 'bleak CEO outlook' for 2023
Date: Mon, 16 Jan 2023 17:45:05 GMT
URL: https://finance.yahoo.com/news/davos-pw-c-survey-finds-bleak-ceo-outlook-for-2023-174505340.html
Summary:
• PwC survey at Davos finds 'bleak' outlook for 2023, with 73% of CEOs expecting global economic growth to decline over the next 12 months 
• Inflation, macroeconomic volatility, and geopolitical conflict ranked as the top threats to businesses 
• Nearly 40% of CEOs surveyed didn't believe their organizations will be economically viable in 10 years if they do not transform 
• CEOs don't plan to cut deeply from a layoff perspective, with 60% having no intentions to reduce headcount and 80% having no plans to lower compensation 
• S&P Chief U.S. Economist Beth Ann Bovino expects a "shallow" recession to occur later this year.
