Updated: 6:24 PM EST Jan 15, 2023 Jessica Kisluk Digital Editor A popular restaurant in Manchester announced they're officially closing their South Main Street location. The original location of Blake's Restaurant will be closing Saturday, Jan. 21, after nearly 60 years, according to their website. The company said they'll continue to produce ice cream across New England but will no longer have a brick-and-mortar location in Manchester. The Blake’s Restaurant on Hooksett Road closed in January 2021 after almost 40 years in business. The company said they are thankful to the community for all the years of service.Blake's Creamery dates back to 1900. MANCHESTER, N.H. — A popular restaurant in Manchester announced they're officially closing their South Main Street location. The original location of Blake's Restaurant will be closing Saturday, Jan. 21, after nearly 60 years, according to their website.
