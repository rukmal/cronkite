Title: Mortgage rates drop again, creating opportunity for homebuyers: Freddie Mac | Fox Business
Date: Mon, 16 Jan 2023 13:00:27 GMT

Summary:
• Mortgage rates dropped to 6.33%, creating savings opportunities for homebuyers
• Market volatility has caused mortgage rates to fluctuate in the 6% to 7% range since September
• Shopping for the best deal could result in tens of thousands of dollars saved over the loan's life
• Inflation figures have cooled, but consumer credit is rising and pushing consumers to borrow more
• Markets west of the Mississippi are expected to favor buyers by the end of 2023
