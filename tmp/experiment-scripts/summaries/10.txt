Summary:
• Invest in dividend stocks for long-term gains
• Focus on being a buyer, not a seller
• Operate portfolio like a business
• Don't assume you know the future
• Sell when it improves your portfolio
• Withdraw cash flow produced through dividends and interest
• Reinvest a portion of income to buy more dividend stocks
• Buy more of the economy and collect more dividends
• Don't fund retirement by selling off assets
• Buy stocks with outlook to hold forever
• Diversify to reduce risk of dividend cuts
