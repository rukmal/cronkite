Title: CDC Pfizer bivalent COVID shot link to strokes - The Jerusalem Post
Date: Sun, 15 Jan 2023 16:31:00 GMT
URL: https://www.jpost.com/health-and-wellness/coronavirus/article-728616
Summary:
• CDC and FDA have identified a possible link between Pfizer/BioNTech's bivalent COVID shot and ischemic stroke in people aged 65 and over
• Other monitoring systems have not identified this safety issue
• Pfizer and BioNTech have stated that there is no evidence to conclude that ischemic stroke is associated with their COVID-19 vaccine
