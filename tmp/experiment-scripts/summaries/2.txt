Title: China’s population drops for first time since 1961, highlights demographic crisis | Reuters
Date: Tue, 17 Jan 2023 03:55:00 GMT
URL: https://www.reuters.com/world/china/chinas-population-shrinks-first-time-since-1961-2023-01-17/
Summary:
• China's population dropped for the first time since 1961, highlighting a demographic crisis
• Birth rate was the lowest on record at 6.77 births per 1,000 people
• Death rate was the highest since 1974 at 7.37 deaths per 1,000 people
• One-child policy and sky-high education costs have put many Chinese off having children
• India is expected to become the world's most populous nation this year
• China's population is expected to shrink by 109 million by 2050
• Government has rolled out measures to encourage people to have more babies
