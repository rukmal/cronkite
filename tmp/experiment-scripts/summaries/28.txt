Title: Wyoming lawmakers propose ban on electric vehicle sales | The Hill
Date: Mon, 16 Jan 2023 20:18:00 GMT

Summary:
• Wyoming: Propose ban on electric vehicle sales by 2035 
• Bill states electric vehicles will hinder Wyoming’s ability to trade with other states 
• Bill encourages citizens and industries not to purchase electric vehicles before ban 
• Bill states oil industry has employed thousands of people in the state 
• 15 other states have moved to ban gas-powered vehicle sales
