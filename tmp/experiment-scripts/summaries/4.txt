Title: Global recession likely, say 63% of chief economists in WEF survey | Fox Business
Date: Mon, 16 Jan 2023 22:06:18 GMT
URL: https://www.foxbusiness.com/economy/global-recession-likely-among-63-percent-chief-economists-survey
Summary:
• 63% of chief economists surveyed by WEF predict global recession.
• Survey results indicate most pessimistic outlook for Europe, with 68% predicting "very weak" growth.
• US expected to have moderate inflation in 2023, according to survey.
• Cost of living and energy crisis expected to be less severe at end of 2023.
• Labor markets outlook split, with 41% predicting tight markets and 41% predicting otherwise.
