Title: An Average 1,600 Tech Jobs Have Been Axed Every Day of 2023 so Far
Date: Mon, 16 Jan 2023 06:18:00 GMT
URL: https://www.businessinsider.com/tech-workers-laid-off-every-day-january-recession-downturn-2023-1
Summary:
• 91 tech companies have axed 24,151 jobs in 15 days of 2023
• 154,256 workers were laid off by 1,023 tech companies in 2022
• Amazon, Meta, and Salesforce top the list of layoffs with 18,000, 11,000, and 8,000 staffers laid-off respectively
• Layoffs in the tech sector show no signs of abating
