Title: More changes to retirement accounts, rules coming in 2024 - oregonlive.com
Date: Mon, 16 Jan 2023 15:47:00 GMT
URL: https://www.oregonlive.com/advice/2023/01/more-changes-to-retirement-accounts-rules-coming-in-2024.html
Summary:
• Employers can count student loan payments when matching retirement funds in an employer-sponsored 401(k)
• Age to withdraw money from retirement savings account pushed back to 73
• Penalty-free withdrawal of up to $1,000 from 401(k) and IRA accounts for certain financial emergencies
• Rollover of up to $35,000 from 529 educational savings accounts into a Roth IRA account
• Automatic enrollment into employer-sponsored retirement plans and emergency savings accounts in 2025
• Maximum annual contributions and health savings accounts limits in 2023
• Higher cost-of-living adjustment for Social Security and cheaper premiums and deductibles for Medicare Part B
