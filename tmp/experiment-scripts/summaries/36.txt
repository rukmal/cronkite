Title: South Carolina: 225 Upstate workers told Timken plant closing
Date: Tue, 17 Jan 2023 00:03:00 GMT
URL: https://www.wyff4.com/article/timken-plant-closing-gaffney-south-carolina/42524590
Summary:

- Timken Company notified 225 workers at its Gaffney, South Carolina bearings facility that the plant will be closing and winding down operations by the end of the year
- Timken has been in Gaffney for 50 years, helping to diversify the economy
- Timken is offering support, benefits planning, severance pay, and the opportunity to apply for open positions at other Timken locations
- The company is giving employees at least 60 days' notice prior to their last day
