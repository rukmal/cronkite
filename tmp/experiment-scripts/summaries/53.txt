Title: Why is the bitcoin (BTC) price rallying in January 2023?
Date: Mon, 16 Jan 2023 15:38:49 GMT
URL: https://www.cnbc.com/2023/01/16/why-is-bitcoin-btc-rallying-in-january.html
Summary:
• BTC price rising to $21,000 for first time since Nov. 7
• Optimism due to potential for Fed to cut interest rates
• Increased probability of interest rates being lowered
• Purchases by large buyers (“whales”)
• Bitcoin mining difficulty increasing
• Miners offloading holdings to pay off debts
• Difficulty reaching record 37.6 trillion
• Bitcoin halving event in 2024 could lead to further price increases
