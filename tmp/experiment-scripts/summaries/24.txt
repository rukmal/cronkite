Title: Gold prices seen rising towards record highs as rate rises near end | Reuters
Date: Mon, 16 Jan 2023 15:51:00 GMT
URL: https://www.reuters.com/markets/commodities/gold-prices-seen-rising-towards-record-highs-rate-rises-near-end-2023-01-16/
Summary:
• Gold prices rising towards record highs above $2,000/oz
• U.S. Federal Reserve slowing rate hikes and eventually stopping
• U.S. dollar weakening and bond yields dropping
• Central banks stockpiling gold and retail demand for gold bars and coins increasing
• Speculators betting gold prices will rise, pushing up prices
• Potential for gold prices to move above $2,100/oz by year-end
